# Working Brand Direction

## Working name

**SproutScout**

Meaning:

- `Sprout` suggests young children and growth.
- `Scout` suggests finding the right place before the family leaves home.

Suggested tagline:

> Your family’s best day, planned.

Alternative product-line copy:

- Three great ideas for right now.
- Less searching. More family time.
- Plans that fit the weather, the nap, and the budget.

## Naming caution

This is a project code name, not a cleared trademark. Before publishing or spending on design:

1. search federal and state trademarks;
2. search Apple App Store and Google Play;
3. search domains and social handles;
4. search company and product databases;
5. review pronunciation and unintended meanings;
6. obtain professional legal clearance.

The earlier name `LittleDay` should not be used because an active childcare/daycare software product is already using it.

## Voice

- calm, practical, and trustworthy;
- never guilt parents;
- avoid exaggerated “perfect parent” language;
- label uncertainty;
- prefer “Here are three good fits” over “This is definitely the best”; 
- use US English and familiar units.

## Visual direction

- warm, modern, uncluttered;
- parent-facing rather than cartoon-heavy child UI;
- map and cards should prioritize decision facts;
- one accent color plus accessible neutrals;
- icons should supplement labels, not replace them.
